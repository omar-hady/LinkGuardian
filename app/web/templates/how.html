How LinkGuardian Works

LinkGuardian analyzes URLs with layered signals. We do not execute arbitrary scripts or crawl content beyond safe HTML parsing.

  • Heuristics: length, subdomains, path depth, query density, keywords, digits and hyphens.
  • Security: HTTPS presence and TLS certificate checks (expiry and SAN match).
  • Domain context: age estimation and suspicious TLDs, IDN/mixed scripts, brand similarity.
  • Threat intelligence: background feeds (OpenPhish, URLHaus) influence scoring without exposing feed names.
  • Network: response time and redirect hints.
  • Deep analysis: optional safe fetch for forms, iframes, and external script counts.

The score is normalized to 0–1 and mapped to: Legit, Suspicious, or Phishing, with the top reasons shown.
